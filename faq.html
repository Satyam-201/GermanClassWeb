Home FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about our courses, tuition, study abroad services and visa process.

Courses

Language Courses & Tuition

Do you offer both online and offline classes?
Yes. Every language course, test-prep batch and tuition subject is available as live online sessions or in-person classroom batches at our centre — you can even switch formats mid-course if your schedule changes.
Do I get a certificate after completing a language course?
Yes, all students receive an institute certificate plus a level-wise language completion certificate, both recognised by our partner universities abroad.
What is the batch size for tuition and language classes?
We cap every batch at 15 students so trainers can give individual attention and everyone gets real speaking or problem-solving practice.
Can I switch from an offline to an online batch later?
Yes, subject to seat availability in the corresponding online batch for your current level.
Study Abroad & Visa

Applications & Visa Process

Which countries do you provide study abroad guidance for?
We guide students for Germany, Canada, UK, Australia, USA, France and Ireland, covering university shortlisting, admissions, scholarships and visa filing.
How early should I start the study abroad process?
Ideally 9-12 months before your intended intake, so there's enough time for language certification, university applications, and visa processing without last-minute pressure.
Do you help with education loans?
Yes, our counsellors help you compare lenders, prepare documentation and understand collateral and interest-rate options for education loans.
What is your visa success rate?
Our overall visa success rate is 98% across all seven countries, driven mainly by careful documentation review before filing.
Is career counselling free?
Yes, the first career counselling and profile evaluation session is completely free with no obligation to enrol.
