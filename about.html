Built by educators, run like an institute students trust

Global Language Academy started with one belief: language training and study-abroad guidance shouldn't be handled by two different, disconnected people. We do both, under one roof.

Our Story

From one classroom to a full study-abroad pathway

Global Language Academy began as a small German-language classroom for engineering graduates who needed B1 certification for university applications. Students kept asking the same question after their exam: "now what about my visa?"

That question became our second department — study abroad counselling and visa assistance — so a student's journey from their first German word to their airport departure stays with one accountable team.

Today we also run academic tuition for Class 11 & 12 Science students who want the same disciplined, small-batch approach for their boards and entrances.
